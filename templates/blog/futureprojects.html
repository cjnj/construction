Micro Roof Gardens

Empire Con. is proud to collaborate with the city and commercial property owners in developing micro roof gardens, transforming urban spaces into lush, green sanctuaries. These innovative projects aim to enhance the city's landscape, creating environmentally friendly and sustainable environments that benefit both the community and the ecosystem. By incorporating these green spaces into rooftops, we are not only improving air quality and reducing urban heat but also promoting biodiversity and providing residents with beautiful, natural areas to enjoy. Empire Con. is dedicated to leading the way in eco-friendly urban development, ensuring a greener future for our city.

New Lower East Side Park

The first phase of construction closures which included East River Park from Stanton Street south to Montgomery Street, began on December 6, 2021. On December 18, 2023, the transition into phase two began with the closure of three additional ballfields to East Houston Street. The team is maintaining the 42% open space and will begin to reopen phase one amenities later in 2024.
